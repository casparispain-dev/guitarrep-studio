What is ChordRep
Chord changes are what separate a beginner from someone who sounds like they can actually play. ChordRep is a chord change trainer. The goal is simple: switch between two chords as many times as you can before the timer runs out. One minute. One pair.

The more you drill them, the smoother and faster they get — without thinking about it.
How to use it
  1. Go to Chords and select the chords you want to drill. Tap as many chords as you like to add to your pool.
  2. Come back to Drill. Two chords are shown side by side — those are your first pair.
  3. Press Start. A 4-beat countdown beeps you in, then the timer runs.
  4. Play your first chord, then switch to the second, then back and forth until the timer runs out.
  5. Each full switch counts as one change. The app will count the number of changes automatically.
  6. Hit Random any time to get a fresh pair.

Mic tip: Enable your mic in Settings and the app detects each strum for hands-free counting.
