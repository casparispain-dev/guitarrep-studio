ChordRep is a focused, mic-driven chord transition trainer. Players pick a chord combination, press start, and practise switching between chords while the app counts their strums via microphone. The goal is to build muscle memory for chord changes through short, repeatable daily drills.

The 60-second drill

  • Player selects a chord combination (2 chords free · 3–4 chords Pro)
  • Player taps Start
  • A silent visual countdown 4 → 3 → 2 → 1 appears on screen
  • A beep signals the drill has begun
  • Player switches between chords — app counts strums via microphone
  • A second beep (different pitch) signals time is up
  • App displays result inline: stars earned and strum count

Two versions

Free
  • 9 open chords — A, Am, C, D, Dm, E, Em, F6, G
  • 1 intermediate taster chord of your choice
  • 2-chord combo drills
  • Stars & strum count per session
Pro
  • Full chord library — all levels
  • 3 and 4-chord combo drills
  • RhythmRep link — BPM auto-increments during drills
  • App-to-app integration with other GuitarRep apps
